Our Story

A culinary journey that began with a simple dream

Founded in 1985 by Chef Marco Bella, Bella Vista has been serving authentic Italian cuisine with a modern twist for over three decades. Our commitment to using only the finest ingredients and traditional cooking methods has made us a beloved destination for food enthusiasts.

Every dish is crafted with love and attention to detail, ensuring that each visit is a memorable culinary experience. From our handmade pasta to our wood-fired pizzas, we bring the authentic flavors of Italy to your table.
